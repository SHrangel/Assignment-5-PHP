The Lion King

  • Info:

    The Lion King (2019) is a movie about a young lion prince named Simba who must embrace his role as the rightful king of his homeland following the murder of his father, Mufasa, at the hands of his uncle, Scar. The movie is set in Africa, where Simba is the pride and joy of his parents King Mufasa and Queen Sarabi. After the murder of his father, Simba flees his kingdom only to learn the true meaning of responsibility and bravery.
